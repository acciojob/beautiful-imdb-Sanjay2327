Set in the continents of Westeros and Essos, Game of Thrones follows the violent dynastic struggles among noble houses for the Iron Throne. The story interweaves three main arcs: the political scheming of families like the Starks and Lannisters; the rise of the exiled Daenerys Targaryen as she gathers an army and hatches three dragons; and the looming threat of the White Walkers, an ancient undead army marching from the frozen North.
